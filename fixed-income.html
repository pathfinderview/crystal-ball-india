Why fixed income earns a seat at the table

Stability compounding

Debt instruments reduce drawdowns—helping long‑term compounding survive bad months.

Known cashflows

FDs and bonds offer predictable income; duration and credit quality can be tuned to your goals.

Behavioural ballast

A calm base cuts the urge to “chase” headlines—protecting returns from your own worst impulses.

Pick your calm

Bank FDs

Simple, insured up to limits, rate‑locked. Ladder maturities to reduce reinvestment risk.

High‑quality Bonds / G‑Secs

Credit‑safe and liquid. Match duration to horizon; use T‑Bills/short G‑Secs for parking, longer G‑Secs for stability with duration risk.

Debt Mutual Funds

Access diversified portfolios (liquid/ultra‑short/short‑term). Check credit quality, duration, and exit loads.
