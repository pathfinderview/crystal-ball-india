Why this matters for your plan

Headlines ≠ outcomes

Markets often move opposite to the “obvious” read—because positioning and liquidity dominate the last mile.

Volatility hits behaviour

Losses cluster; people sell low, buy high. Systems and rules help—so does a calm base of fixed income.

Compounding likes stability

Drawdowns are a tax. Capping downside (with debt/fixed income) keeps compounding intact.
