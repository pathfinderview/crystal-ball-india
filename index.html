Welcome to the Crystal Ball — India

This is a simple, serious exercise in decision‑making. You’ll read real, time‑stamped headlines as they appeared and make one clean wealth call for that date. We then settle your result against the NIFTY 50 close for that day (vs the prior close) and let compounding tell the story.

You will be trading the NIFTY 50 index with a fixed, unlevered exposure. Your choice each round is simply UP or DOWN (or Skip). Headlines are shown unmasked—just like you would actually read them—covering RBI decisions, IMD monsoon updates, budget cues, policy, and global macro.

Your starting wealth is ₹1,000,000.

We focus on learning through compounding, not clever tricks. The set includes months where the “obvious” read would have been wrong.

The ground rules

  1. Dates are sampled from the last 13 months across RBI/IMD/policy/global mixes. No cherry‑picking to deceive.
  2. One clean decision per item: UP / DOWN / Skip. No leverage, no transaction costs.
  3. Settlement uses NIFTY 50 close‑to‑close for that date. Your bank moves by that day’s % in the direction you chose.
  4. Metrics we show as you play: Right/Wrong, average monthly return, and an annualised return (CAGR proxy based on decisions taken).
  5. Transparency: headlines carry a source and link (e.g., Reuters). We never mask directional words.
  6. Educational only. No advice, no ads, no fees.
