How it works

1) See the news (spoiler‑free)

You get a real, time‑stamped front page or headline, with any “markets up/down” text blacked out.

Fairness: no spoilers
2) Make one big call

Pick UP or DOWN (simple mode), or set positions on NIFTY 50 and 10Y G‑Sec (pro mode) with caps on leverage.

Skip allowed
3) See your results

We settle at that day’s close against the prior close. No fees, no financing. You’ll track P&L, hit rate, and average daily return.

FAQ

What sources do you use?

For India, neutral headlines from reliable outlets and official releases; “spoiler” words are filtered out.

How many days?

We’ll start with ~15–30 historical rounds so you can play in one go (no daily drops).

Is this investing advice?

No. This is an educational game about decision‑making under uncertainty.
